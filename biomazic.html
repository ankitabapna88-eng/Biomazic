Topic 12 — Molecular Basis of Inheritance
Class XII · Genetics · Hard
The Hook — 2 min
"In 1953, Watson and Crick stared at an X-ray photo taken by Rosalind Franklin — and in that moment, understood the secret of life. Today you will understand what they saw — and why it changed everything."
Core Concept — DNA Structure
Right-handed B-form double helix. Antiparallel strands. A=T (2 H-bonds), G≡C (3 H-bonds). Width = 2nm, Pitch = 3.4nm, 10 bp per turn, rise per bp = 0.34nm.
⚠ NEET TRAP: Pitch (3.4nm) ≠ Width (2nm) ≠ Rise per bp (0.34nm). All three tested separately every year.
Pro Trick
"UAA = U-Are-Away (Ochre). UAG = U-Are-Gone (Amber). UGA = U-Go-Away (Opal). Never forget all three stop codons again."
